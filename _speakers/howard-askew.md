    Howard Askew is a Senior Data Technologist at the ODI. He is an enthusiastic champion of data literacy and the smart, ethical use of data and technology to drive positive changes. He supports the ODI's consultancy team, most recently as Technical Lead on the Department for Business and Trade's Smart Data Challenge Prize, and leads on the development and delivery of data standards and open data initiatives such as OpenActive.
