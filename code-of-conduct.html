This Code of Conduct applies to all Dundee Data Meetup activities, including in-person events and online communities (such as Slack).

1. Respect and Inclusivity

  • Treat all participants with respect, kindness, and courtesy, regardless of their background, experience, or perspectives.
  • Foster an inclusive environment that welcomes and values individuals from diverse cultures, ethnicities, genders, sexual orientations, and abilities.
  • Avoid any form of discrimination, harassment, or offensive behaviour, including but not limited to verbal, written, or physical actions in both online and offline interactions.
  • Be mindful that online communications can be misinterpreted; choose your words carefully and assume positive intent from others.

2. Professional and Respectful Communication

  • Maintain a professional and courteous demeanour in all interactions, whether in-person or online.
  • Use appropriate language and avoid profanity, offensive jokes, or inappropriate content.
  • In online communities, stay on-topic in designated channels and use thread replies when appropriate to keep discussions organized.
  • Respect others' time by avoiding excessive posting, spamming, or derailing conversations.

3. Collaboration and Knowledge Sharing

  • Encourage constructive and meaningful discussions, promoting the sharing of knowledge, insights, and experiences.
  • Engage in open-minded conversations, embracing different perspectives and fostering a culture of learning and growth.
  • Respect the privacy and confidentiality of others when discussing sensitive topics or sharing information.
  • When sharing resources or links in online communities, ensure they are relevant and appropriate for the discussion.

4. Online Community Guidelines

  • Use your real name or a professional handle that identifies you to the community.
  • Do not share personal contact information of other members without their explicit consent.
  • Avoid direct messaging community members for unsolicited promotional content or job offers unless explicitly welcomed.
  • Report spam, inappropriate content, or violations to moderators promptly.
  • Respect channel purposes and community guidelines specific to each online platform.

5. Compliance with Policies

  • Adhere to all applicable laws, regulations, and policies related to data privacy and security.
  • Respect the intellectual property rights of others and avoid unauthorized use or distribution of copyrighted materials.
  • Do not share confidential or proprietary information from your workplace or other organizations.

6. Reporting and Enforcement

  • Report any violations of the Code of Conduct to the event organizers or contact us directly at dundeedatameetup@gmail.com.
  • All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.
  • Organizers will address reported incidents appropriately, which may include warnings, temporary suspensions from online communities, or removal from events and community spaces.
  • For online communities, moderators may remove inappropriate content and take immediate action to maintain a safe environment.

By participating in Dundee Data Meetup events and online communities, you agree to uphold and abide by this Code of Conduct. We are committed to creating a positive, inclusive, and professional community both online and offline.

If you have any concerns or need to report a violation of the Code of Conduct, please contact us at dundeedatameetup@gmail.com. We will address all issues promptly and confidentially.
